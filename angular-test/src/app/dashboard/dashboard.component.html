Recent Innovations in Clean Energy

Solar-Powered Trains

Solar energy is a great way of keeping energy clean and preventing the complete
collapse of our atmosphere through the nasty and dirty air we alow to infiltrate
our ozone. This is a form of transportation that can go all day without the use
of coal or electricity. Trains usually emit a lot of air pollution so the invention of
solar-powered trains will be better as it will take energy directly from the sun to stay powered.

Bladeless Wind Energy

We are used to seeing spining blades when seeing different wind turbines, but how about a wind
turbine that doesn't use spinning blades, but does the exact same job? The reason why this
would be incredibly useful is due to the fact that it will be effecient in low winds as they will
be able to operate with littel to no wind. This will also allow the communities to
generate renewable energy directly.

"Rechargable" Tires

Rechargable tires are extremely useful as they are trying to be made as a very adaptable

All in all, these are the latest inventions revolutionizing the world of clean energy.
Each innovation, from solar-powered trains to bladeless wind energy and rechargeable tires, represents a significant step toward reducing our dependence on non-renewable energy sources.
